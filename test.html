System Architecture

Adaptive Comfort

Electronic calibration for ride refinement.

Variable Steering: Adjusts EPAS motor assistance based on speed to reduce driver fatigue.

Pliant Damping: Calibrates ESC to absorb road chatter for a smoother chassis response.

Grade Braking

Transmission-led thermal management.

Downshift Logic: Proactively holds lower gears during descents to use engine compression.

Fade Prevention: Reduces reliance on physical pads, keeping brakes cool for emergency use.

Rear Diff Lock

Mechanical axle synchronization.

50:50 Split: Forces both wheels to turn at identical speeds, ensuring power even if one wheel is lifted.

Traction Logic: Crucial for recovery in deep mud or sand where surface friction is non-existent.
