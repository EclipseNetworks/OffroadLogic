System Architecture

Adaptive Comfort

Electronic calibration of steering weight and suspension response to minimize driver fatigue on long-distance routes.

Variable Steering

Increases assistance at low speeds for maneuverability and firms up at highway speeds for stability.

Pliant Damping

Softens chassis reactions to road imperfections, providing a luxury-sedan feel in a rugged pickup frame.

Grade Braking

Utilizes 10-speed transmission logic to hold gears on descents, using engine compression to slow the vehicle.

Thermal Protection

Prevents brake fade by reducing reliance on physical brake pads during heavy towing or steep declines.

Proactive Logic

Monitors brake pedal pressure and angle; automatically downshifts if the system senses the driver needs more control.

Diff Locking

Mechanically links both rear wheels to rotate at identical speeds, providing maximum torque to wheels with traction.

Maximum Traction

Essential for recovery; ensures that even if one wheel is off the ground, the other continues to drive the truck forward.

Surface Warning

Should only be engaged on loose surfaces (mud, sand, gravel) to avoid driveline binding and tire scrubbing on pavement.
