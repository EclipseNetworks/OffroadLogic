Tactical Drive States

MTS: Sand

Disables TRC (Traction Control) to prevent engine bog-down. Optimizes gear hunting for consistent high-RPM beach transit.

A-TRC Logic

Active Traction Control applies brake force to spinning wheels, transferring torque to those with contact. Effective in cross-axle mogul situations.

Crawl Control

Maintains a constant low speed without driver pedal input. Essential for self-extraction in soft sand or deep mud.

DAC System

Downhill Assist Control manages speed on steep technical descents. Prevents wheel lock-up while maintaining steering control.
