6 Selectable Modes

Normal Mode

Perfect for everyday driving. Balanced power and steering for effortless cruising.

Default: 2H / 4A

Eco Mode

Maximizes fuel efficiency by tuning throttle response and optimizing gear shifts.

Default: 2H

Tow/Haul

Designed for heavy loads. Delivers power at higher RPMs and uses engine braking.

Default: 4A

Slippery

Adjusts traction control for ice, wet roads, or loose gravel to prevent wheel spin.

Default: 4A / 4H

Mud / Ruts

Engages rear locker automatically. Keeps wheels spinning to clear mud from treads.

Default: 4H + Rear Locker

Sand

Maintains high momentum in soft sand through aggressive power delivery.

Default: 4H
